The North Shore National Ice Hockey Officials Association chapter of officials is made up of 60 dedicated members. These men and women have chosen to make the commitment to uphold what it means to be a NIHOA official. The membership; who’s expertise on the ice can be found in high school, junior and college hockey, are some of the best referees and linesman the North Shore of Massachusetts has to offer.

We are proud to be participating in this year’s high school season and will continue to help build the skills of players through the enforcement of the rules of the game.

The North Shore chapter of officials will also continue to give back to the hockey community anyway it can through the generosity of it’s members.

North Shore Chapter is a part of the National Ice Hockey Officials Association - 2014
